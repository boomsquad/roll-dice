Rolling Dice

This program is going to simulate the street game of rolling dice. Two dice are rolled and the total is added up. If you roll a total of 7 or 11 you win. If you roll the same number two times in a row you win. If you roll a two you roll again. If you roll a 7 or 11, "you win!". If you roll a 2 you get to roll again. Lastly, if you roll the same number two times in a row, "you win!"
